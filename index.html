What is a Treasury Bill?
Short term investment product offered by the Bank of Ghana on behalf of the Government. Purchasing a treasury bill is lending money to the Government.

What interest do I earn on my T-Bill?
Interest on all T-bills are decided by Bank of Ghana every week. The interest to be applied shall be the current interest rate on the day of purchase.

What is the charge?
You pay absolutely nothing when you buy treasury bills through I4M. It is free of charge.

What are the benefits?

  • Treasury bills are risk free.
  • They can easily be converted into cash.
  • Interest earned is higher than bank savings account rates.
  • No transaction cost. You do not pay anything.
  • They can be used as collateral for a loan.
  • Statements can be used when applying for visas.

What minimum amount do I/we need to start?
Five Ghana Cedis (GH¢ 5.00) only and thereafter in multiples of five Ghana Cedis (GH¢ 5.00).
